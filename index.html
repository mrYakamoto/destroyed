ABOUT THIS SITE HERE

Before you can build the house back up, you have to burn it to the ground. This site is a resource for classic and upcoming films of devastating emotional heft. It's easy for our brains to trick us into thinking our problems are the most important things that've ever happened. In times like this, the power of film can serve as a powerful reminder that we're all but specs of dust in an eternal sea of mystery. Suddenly, our puny issues don't seem quite so bad.
